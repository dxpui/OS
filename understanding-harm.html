RISK 1 - UNDERSTANDING HARMS

Understanding risk factors and harms that might affect your service

Get started

Useful information before you start this module

30 min - 1 hour module (depending on complexity of your service)

The Online Safety Act 2023 requires that your risk assessment assess the risk of each kind of illegal harm set out in the Act. The priority illegal content defined by the Act includes many individual offences, and Ofcom has grouped these into kinds of illegal harm

This is the first step of the risk assessment process and will:

  • Help you understand which kinds of illegal harm are most likely to occur on your service
  • Help you understand which risk factors may play a role

Who Should Complete the module? 

Anyone with a full understanding of what your service does, any features that it has and who understands the user base of your service

How long does this module take, and when does it need to be completed? 

*This module will take approximately 30 minutes to an hour, depending on how complex your service is.
